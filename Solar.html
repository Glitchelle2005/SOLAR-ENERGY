SOLAR ENERGY

Solar Energy is a renewable source of energy obtained from the sun. It is clean, sustainable, and helps reduce environmental pollution. Solar energy converts sunlight into electricity using solar panels.

Solar Energy Illustration

Advantages of Solar Energy

  • Renewable and Sustainable
  • Reduces Electricity Bills
  • Environmentally Friendly
  • Low Maintenance Cost

How Solar Energy Works

  1. Sunlight hits solar panels
  2. Panels convert sunlight into electricity
  3. Electricity is used in homes
  4. Extra energy is stored or sent to the grid
